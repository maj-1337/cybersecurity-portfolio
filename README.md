## NCL CTF Experience
Participating in the NCL's Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges was a valuable learning experience that helped strengthen my problem-solving, analytical thinking, and cybersecurity skills. Through hands-on challenges involving cryptography, web security, networking, and forensics and more. I gained practical exposure to real-world security concepts and tools. The experience also improved my ability to work under pressure, research independently, and think creatively when approaching technical problems. Overall, the CTF experience increased my confidence and deepened my interest in pursuing a career in cybersecurity.
